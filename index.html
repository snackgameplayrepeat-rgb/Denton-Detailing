Meet Micah

Denton Detailing is a one-person operation — when you book, you know exactly who's working on your car.

My approach

I specialize in auto detailing that fits real life — daily drivers, family haulers, work trucks, everything in between. I focus on what actually matters: clean carpets, fresh interiors, streak-free glass, and a finish that makes you proud to drive again.

Every appointment is booked directly with me. I keep my schedule intentionally small so each car gets the time it deserves.
