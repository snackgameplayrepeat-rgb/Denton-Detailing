About me Meet Micah

Denton Detailing is a one-person, detail-obsessed operation. You're not just a time slot—you're a neighbor, and your car deserves the same care you'd give it if you had all day.

I'm Micah, and I specialize in professional auto detailing that fits real life—daily drivers, family haulers, work vehicles, and everything in between. I focus on the details that actually matter: clean carpets, fresh interiors, streak-free glass, and a finish that makes you proud to drive again.

Every appointment is booked directly with me, so you always know who's working on your vehicle. I keep my schedule intentionally limited so I can give each car the time and attention it deserves.
